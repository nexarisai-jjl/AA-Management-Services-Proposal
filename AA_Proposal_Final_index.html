The AI Agent in Action

A High-Level Workflow Breakdown

1

Step 1: Document Ingestion

Subcontractor bills or customer invoice sheets (Excel/PDF) are automatically detected and ingested when dropped into a designated cloud folder (e.g., Google Drive, OneDrive) or received via a specific email address, triggering the automation.

2

Step 2: Intelligent Data Extraction

The AI agent reads the incoming file. It directly parses Excel documents and utilizes advanced OCR for PDFs. A Large Language Model then intelligently extracts all relevant line items and header information, normalizes the data, and flags any potential ambiguities or missing fields for human review.

3

Step 3: QuickBooks Payload Generation

Based on the extracted and normalized data, the AI agent constructs a preliminary QuickBooks Online Bill or Invoice payload, accurately mapping vendor/customer references, work order numbers, dates, descriptions, amounts, and relevant accounting categories.

4

Step 4: Human-in-the-Loop Review

The preliminary QuickBooks entry is created in QuickBooks Online as a draft or pending transaction. Your staff can then review, make any necessary edits directly within QuickBooks, and verify the accuracy of the extracted and mapped data, ensuring quality control using the familiar QBO interface.

5

Step 5: One-Click Posting & Audit Trail

With a single click from within QuickBooks, the approved entry is securely posted. The original document is automatically attached to the transaction, and a detailed audit trail is logged, recording approvals, changes, and timestamps for full transparency and compliance.
